Short Info About Let us Think Green Project

The objective of the " Let us Think Green” Project is to raise awareness and consciousness about the importance of keeping a clean, green and sustainable environment including clean water sources, atmosphere, the seas and beaches in the project countries and the EU.

5 Non-governmental organizations from different regions of Europe (TEK-SAV-Turkiye, Koinoniki Anaptyksi Neon / K.A.NE- Greece, Kalistratia OdV - Italy, Asociacija TAVO Europa -Lithuania and Walk Together Association - Bulgaria) are  collaborating to achieve the objectives outlined in the European Union's Green Deal’s targets.

Project has 4 work packages , Project  started on 01 February 2023 and will finish on 31 January 2025.  All project outputs and materials  will be published on the project web site.

Please follow us from web sites and social media and send your opinion and recommendations.

Let us build a green  and clean  environment to live healthier and happier in the world.

Association WalkTogether

Association WalkTogether (WT) is created in 2013 in Sofia - Bulgaria to serves as a learning organization that gathers trainers, youth learner, youth policy makers, experts, and youth volunteers with different levels of competences to actively be involved in the creation of civic activities with European dimension and with benefits for their local community.
